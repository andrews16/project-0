This is a tool for proctors, chairpeople, presentors, teachers, and meeting leaders everywhere. The sleek and simple website assists with all of you pre-meeting preparations. - Stay on time with a live clock. - During that 15-minutes before the start of you meeting, instead of scratching down your talking points on a notebook, type them out here. - Keep all your nessecary links one click away by adding them to the screen. - You can generate an icebreaker question for small get-to-know eachother meetings. You can randomly get a food category if you're scheduling a larger lunch meeting.
